Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 17, 2026

ScreenGeni.us ("we", "us", or "our") respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect when you visit screengeni.us or contact us through this site, how we use it, and the choices you have.

Information we collect

We only collect the information you choose to give us. When you submit our contact or demo-request form, we collect the details you enter, which may include:

  • Your name
  • Your company
  • Your email address
  • Your phone number (optional)

We do not require you to create an account, and we do not knowingly collect information from children.

How we use your information

We use the information you provide to:

  • Respond to your inquiry and schedule demos
  • Communicate with you about our products and services
  • Improve our website and offerings

We do not sell your personal information.

How form submissions are handled

Our contact form is processed by Web3Forms, a third-party service that delivers your message to us by email. Your submission is handled according to Web3Forms' own privacy practices in addition to this policy.

Cookies & analytics

This site uses minimal cookies necessary for basic functionality. If we use analytics tools to understand how visitors use the site, those tools may collect aggregated, non-identifying usage data. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

Data retention & security

We keep the information you submit only as long as needed to respond to your request and for legitimate business purposes. We take reasonable measures to protect your information, though no method of transmission over the internet is completely secure.

Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to access, correct, or delete the personal information we hold about you. To make a request, contact us using the details below.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
