About ScreenGeni.us

We help people find
what they actually mean.

ScreenGeni.us is an AI-powered discovery platform for content, commerce, and culture. We help organizations turn static catalogs, archives, media libraries, and inventory into conversational experiences that guide people from intent to action.

Instead of forcing users to search by exact keywords or filters, ScreenGeni.us lets them ask naturally, refine by taste and context, and discover the right result faster.

Why we exist

Discovery is changing.

People no longer want to dig through endless lists, filters, and search results. They expect intelligent experiences that understand what they are looking for, even when they describe it by mood, use case, vibe, taste, or meaning.

But most organizations are still relying on keyword search, tags, and static navigation. That creates a gap between what users want and what platforms can surface.

ScreenGeni.us was built to close that gap.

What we build

An AI discovery layer for your existing content.

ScreenGeni.us sits between user intent and discovery. We connect to the content, catalog, archive, or inventory an organization already owns, then create an intelligent layer that helps users find the right result through conversation, semantic search, and personalized recommendations.

We are not trying to replace your platform. We make your platform easier to explore.

Taste Profiling
Understand user preferences quickly, even before there is a long history of behavior.
Semantic Search
Match on meaning, context, and relationships — not just keywords.
Conversational Discovery
Let users refine naturally without restarting their search.
Analytics & Insights
Help teams understand what people are asking for, where discovery breaks down, and what content or inventory is being surfaced.
